### T2.2 — AL-ṬABARĪ — TĀRĪKH AL-RUSUL WAL-MULŪK
**Full title:** Tārīkh al-Rusul wal-Mulūk  
**Author:** Imām Abū Jaʿfar Muḥammad ibn Jarīr al-Ṭabarī (d. 310 AH / 923 CE)  
**Arabic title:** تاريخ الرسل والملوك  
**Available in:** Arabic (multiple publishers); English translation (SUNY Press, multiple translators)  
**Use in series:**  
- Alternative historical traditions (e.g., Khadījah's age at marriage)
- Chronological details of pre-Islamic Arabian history
- Background context for political and tribal landscape

### T2.3 — IBN SAʿD — AL-ṬABAQĀT AL-KUBRĀ
**Full title:** Al-Ṭabaqāt al-Kubrā  
**Author:** Abū ʿAbdullah Muḥammad ibn Saʿd al-Baṣrī (d. 230 AH / 845 CE)  
**Arabic title:** الطبقات الكبرى  
**Available in:** Arabic (Dār al-Ṣādir, Beirut)  
**Use in series:**
- Biographical profiles of Companions and Mothers of the Believers
- Details of Khadījah's lineage, children, and prior marriages
- Early Companion profiles for the Profiles tab

### T2.4 — AL-BAYHAQĪ — DALĀIL AL-NUBUWWAH
**Full title:** Dalāʾil al-Nubuwwah wa Maʿrifat Aḥwāl Ṣāḥib al-Sharīʿah  
**Author:** Imām Abū Bakr Aḥmad ibn al-Ḥusayn al-Bayhaqī (d. 458 AH / 1066 CE)  
**Arabic title:** دلائل النبوة ومعرفة أحوال صاحب الشريعة  
**Use in series:**
- Miraculous signs during the Prophet's childhood and youth
- The cloud that followed the Prophet ﷺ on the trade journey (L10)
- Ḥalīmah's blessings during the nursing years (L4)
- Baḥīrā's recognition of the Prophet (L8)

## TIER 3 — SCHOLARLY SYNTHESES AND REFERENCE WORKS

### T3.1 — AL-RAḤĪQ AL-MAKHTŪM (THE SEALED NECTAR)
**Full title:** Al-Raḥīq al-Makhtūm  
**Author:** Ṣafī al-Raḥmān al-Mubārakpūrī (d. 2006)  
**Arabic title:** الرحيق المختوم  
**Publisher:** Darussalam, Riyadh  
**Use in series:** Cross-reference for chronological framework; useful synthesis of classical positions; not used as primary source for any individual claim.  
**Available in:** Arabic, English (Darussalam)

### T3.2 — AL-BIDĀYAH WAL-NIHĀYAH (THE BEGINNING AND THE END)
**Full title:** Al-Bidāyah wal-Nihāyah  
**Author:** Imām Ismāʿīl ibn ʿUmar ibn Kathīr al-Dimashqī (d. 774 AH / 1373 CE)  
**Arabic title:** البداية والنهاية  
**Use in series:** Secondary cross-reference; Ibn Kathīr's evaluations of narration chains provide useful critical context for Tier 2 narrations.

### T3.3 — WHEN THE MOON SPLIT
**Full title:** When the Moon Split: A Biography of the Prophet Muhammad  
**Author:** Ṣafī al-Raḥmān al-Mubārakpūrī  
**Publisher:** Darussalam  
**Use in series:** Accessible English Sīrah overview used for cross-referencing; not primary source. Useful Further Reading recommendation in Companions.

## SOURCES NOT USED (AND WHY)

### Fabricated or Extremely Weak Narrations (Mawḍūʿ)
Not catalogued — these are rejected outright and not referenced in any lesson content.

### Orientalist Academic Biographies
Works such as Montgomery Watt's "Muhammad: Prophet and Statesman" are not used as primary sources. They may be referenced for understanding Western scholarly perspectives but are not cited in Reader content. The series is grounded in the Islamic scholarly tradition.

### Partial or Uncorroborated Online Sources
No online Islamic article or website is used as a primary source. All claims trace back to printed classical works.
