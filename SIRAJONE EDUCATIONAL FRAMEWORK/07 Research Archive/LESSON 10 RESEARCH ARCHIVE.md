## CORE QUESTION THIS LESSON ANSWERS

Who was Khadījah bint Khuwaylid? What was the character of the Prophet ﷺ that caused her to choose him? What was the nature of their marriage? And how did the fifteen years before the Revelation shape the Prophet's readiness for what was to come?

## KEY RESEARCH QUESTIONS AND RESOLUTIONS

### Q1: How old was the Prophet ﷺ at the time of the trade journey?
**Source:** Sīrat Ibn Hishām (Tier 2)  
**Answer:** The classical position is twenty-five years old.  
**Consensus:** Accepted across classical scholarship.  
**Language used:** Stated as historical fact; source noted in Companion Key Facts.

### Q2: How old was Khadījah at the time of marriage?
**Source:** Sīrat Ibn Hishām says forty. Al-Ṭabarī records alternatives of 28 and 25.  
**Resolution:** The forty-year position is the dominant classical position and is followed in this lesson. The existence of alternatives is noted explicitly in Segment 2 and in Companion Key Facts.  
**Language used in Reader:**  "The most widely transmitted classical position is that she is forty years old."  
**Language used in Companion Key Facts:** Full explanation of the dispute with source attribution.

### Q3: Who was Maysarah?
**Source:** Sīrat Ibn Hishām (Tier 2)  
**Answer:** Maysarah was Khadījah's trusted servant/employee who accompanied the Prophet ﷺ on the Syrian trade journey. He reported back to Khadījah his observations of the Prophet's character and the signs he witnessed.  
**Language used:** Presented as a named historical figure whose role is established in the classical Sīrah.

### Q4: The cloud that shaded the Prophet ﷺ — what is the source?
**Source:** Al-Bayhaqī's Dalāʾil al-Nubuwwah and Sīrat Ibn Hishām (Tier 2)  
**Status:** Well-known in the classical Sīrah tradition. Not in Bukhārī or Muslim.  
**Decision:** Included with clear hedging — "Classical sources report that Maysarah observed something remarkable..."  
**Rejected option:** Presenting it as definitively established fact equal to Tier 1.

### Q5: Who was Nafīsah bint Munabbih?
**Source:** Sīrat Ibn Hishām (Tier 2)  
**Answer:** A noble woman of Mecca, trusted by Khadījah, who acted as the intermediary — approaching the Prophet ﷺ on Khadījah's behalf to learn if he would consider marriage.  
**Language used:** Presented as a named historical figure. Her role is explained in Segment 2.

### Q6: The First Revelation — should it appear in this lesson?
**Decision (critical):** NO. The First Revelation belongs to Lesson 11. Lesson 10 ends with anticipation only — the lesson ends at "The Years Before the Dawn" (Segment 4 title is Thabāt — steadfastness during the years of waiting, not revelation). The Closing page's `nextLessonPreview` contains the first hint: "One night in the year 610 CE, in that same cave, he found out. The next lesson begins there — with a voice, an embrace, and five verses that changed the world forever."  
**Why:** Master Principle 7 — The First Revelation Rule. Structural Continuity (Principle 5). The First Revelation must be given its own full lesson. It cannot be used as the climax of a prior lesson.

### Q7: The Memory Gem ḥadīth — which ḥadīth number?
**Source:** Ṣaḥīḥ al-Bukhārī  
**ḥadīth:** The Prophet ﷺ's statement about Khadījah: "She believed in me when people disbelieved me. She assisted me with her wealth when people deprived me. And Allah blessed me with children through her."  
**Correct number:** Ḥadīth 3816  
**Wrong number (rejected):** Ḥadīth 3 (which is ʿĀʾishah's narration of the First Revelation — completely different)  
**Resolution:** This error was identified and corrected in the L10 Companion drafting process. Bukhārī 3816 is the correct citation for the Memory Gem.

### Q8: Was it technically Khadījah's first marriage?
**Sources:** Ibn Saʿd, Al-Ṭabarī (Tier 2)  
**Answer:** Most classical sources indicate she had been married twice before. She was a widow (classical accounts give different names for her previous husbands: Abū Hālah ibn Zunārah and ʿAtīq ibn ʿĀʾidh).  
**Decision:** This was mentioned briefly in the lesson to provide complete historical context. Her previous experience as a businesswoman and her maturity is connected to her wisdom in choosing Muḥammad ﷺ.

### Q9: What is the significance of Surah al-Ḍuḥā as the verse for Segment 4?
**Source:** Qur'ān (Tier 1)  
**Answer:** Surah al-Ḍuḥā was revealed as direct comfort to the Prophet ﷺ during a period of silence from Revelation — after the initial Revelation but before Revelation resumed regularly. Its verses — "Did He not find you an orphan and give shelter? And He found you lost and guided you? And He found you poor and made you self-sufficient?" — look back at Allah's care before the Prophet even knew he was chosen. This makes it the perfect Tier 1 anchor for the theme of "the years before the dawn."

## ACCEPTED NARRATIONS

All narrations used in L10 are from Sīrat Ibn Hishām (Tier 2) unless otherwise noted. Key narrations accepted:
- The Prophet's trade journey to Syria on behalf of Khadījah (Ibn Hishām).
- Maysarah's report to Khadījah and the cloud narration (Ibn Hishām/Bayhaqī).
- Nafīsah's role as intermediary (Ibn Hishām).
- The marriage ceremony and mahr (Ibn Hishām).
- Khadījah's age at marriage — 40 years (dominant classical position).
- The years of domestic peace before Revelation — the taḥannuth (solitary retreat to Ḥirāʾ).

---

## REJECTED NARRATIONS

- Any narration placing the First Revelation within this lesson (belongs to L11).
- Alternative ages for Khadijah (25, 28) are noted as alternatives but not adopted as the main position.
- Any narration suggesting specific details of the years before Revelation that lack clear classical sourcing.
