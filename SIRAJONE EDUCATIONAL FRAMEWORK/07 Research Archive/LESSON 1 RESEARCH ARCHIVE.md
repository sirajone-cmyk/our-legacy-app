## CORE QUESTION THIS LESSON ANSWERS

Why study the Sīrah of the Prophet ﷺ? What makes it different from studying any other historical biography?

---

---

## RESEARCH QUESTIONS RAISED DURING WRITING

1. **What is the difference between Sīrah and biography?**  
   Resolved: Sīrah is not merely historical biography. It is a religious and educational discipline. The narrative of the Prophet's life is a model (uswah) for the believer's conduct. This distinction drives the entire series.

2. **Why does the Sīrah begin before the Prophet's birth?**  
   Resolved: The classical scholars begin with the Arabian context — the Jāhiliyyah period, the political landscape, the state of monotheism — because the context makes the message comprehensible. The opening lesson follows this tradition.

3. **Which Qur'ānic verse best captures the purpose of studying the Sīrah?**  
   Resolved: Surah al-Aḥzāb 33:21 — "Indeed in the Messenger of Allah you have a good example for whoever hopes for Allah and the Last Day." Used as the foundational verse for the entire series.
